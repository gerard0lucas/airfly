National Aviation Research Forum Publications

The International Journal on Aviation, Hospitality, Travel, and Tourism, published by the National Aviation Research Forum (NARF), is a prestigious publication dedicated to advancing knowledge and innovation in these dynamic fields. Supported by AIR ERA, the journal features cutting-edge research, insightful analysis, and practical solutions to contemporary challenges. It serves as a platform for scholars, industry professionals, and policymakers to share their findings and foster collaboration, aiming to shape the future of aviation, hospitality, travel, and tourism through academic excellence and industry relevance.

Online Paper Submission Process for the International Journal on Aviation, Hospitality, Travel, and Tourism

Step 1: Prepare Your Manuscript
  • Ensure your paper is original, unpublished, and not under review elsewhere.
  • Format your manuscript according to the journal's guidelines, including abstract, keywords, references, and figures/tables.

Step 2: Prepare Your Submission Email
  • Compose an email to editor/nationalaviationresearchforum@gmail.com.
  • In the subject line, include “Manuscript Submission: [Your Paper Title]”.

Step 3: Attach Your Manuscript
  • Attach your manuscript file in the specified format (e.g., DOC, DOCX, PDF).
  • Include any additional files, such as figures, tables, and supplementary materials, as separate attachments if necessary.

Step 4: Include Submission Details
  • In the body of the email, provide the following information:
    • Title of your paper
    • Abstract (up to 250 words)
    • Keywords (3-5)
    • Full names and affiliations of all authors
    • Contact details of the corresponding author

Step 5: Review and Send
  • Double-check all attachments and information for accuracy and completeness.
  • Send the email to editor/nationalaviationresearchforum@gmail.com.

Step 6: Receive Confirmation
  • You will receive an email confirmation acknowledging receipt of your manuscript.
  • Your paper will be assigned a unique submission ID for tracking purposes.

Step 7: Track Your Submission
  • Await further communication from the editorial team regarding the review process, required revisions, or the final decision on your paper.
