NARF Membership

Working Professional Membership

Eligibility: Individuals currently employed in the aviation industry or related fields.

Benefits:

  • Access to exclusive industry reports and research publications.
  • Invitations to NARF conferences, workshops, and seminars.
  • Networking opportunities with industry experts and peers.
  • Discounts on NARF events and certification programs.
  • Eligibility to participate in special interest groups and committees.

Fee: 2000 INR (Indian Citizen) / 30 $ (Foreign National)

Mentor Membership

Eligibility:

  • Over 15 years in aviation.
  • Experience in airport operations, airlines, or related fields.

Benefits:

  • Gain recognition as an industry leader.
  • Network with experts and attend exclusive events.
  • Contribute to aviation's future and mentor others.
  • Enjoy personal fulfillment and stay updated on trends.

Role and Responsibilitie:

  • Provide mentoring and career guidance.
  • Conduct workshops and develop educational resources.
  • Collaborate on research and review proposals.
  • Facilitate networking and advocate industry best practices.

Enrolment Process:

  • Complete the online application form on the NARF website.
  • Submit a detailed resume highlighting your experience.
  • Applications reviewed by the NARF mentorship committee.
  • Selected mentors notified within 4-6 weeks.
  • Attend an orientation session and start engaging with mentees.
